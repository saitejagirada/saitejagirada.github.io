Hi there 👋! I'm a Computer Science undergrad at M.V.G.R College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam. My interests are in building efficient and interpretable machine learning systems. I'm currently a Research Intern at NIT Jamshedpur working under Prof. Dinesh Kumar. You can find my projects, my blog and my resume.

At NIT Jamshedpur, I'm designing a FedMARL architecture that integrates federated learning with multi-agent reinforcement learning for distributed decision-making in edge networks. I focus on adaptive aggregation, communication-efficient training, and heterogeneous agent environments. Previously, I've worked on historical document OCR with vision-language models, GPT-style transformers with KV cache optimization, audio event detection, and text classification with GloVe embeddings.
